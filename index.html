CouponIt

Senior Design Project 2018

About Us

CouponIt is a mobile application aiming to transform coupons from an advertising platform to a social networking platform. It will connect users through the sharing of deals and provide users a simple and intuitive way to find deals on specific items quickly with just a scan of an item

Executive Summary

Searching for coupons and deals on a specific item can be time consuming. It is often strenuous to have to manually flip through numerous pages in a booklet or fliers or tedious to scroll through pages on a website to find a specific deal that suits one’s needs. CouponIt aims to solve this by making deals easier and more efficient to find by having coupons readily available through allowing users to find deals on specific items.

CouponIt will benefit the budget conscious consumers as well as businesses. CouponIt’s scanner function will make finding deals possible at the press of a button. Deals or coupons will be instantly available for the user by scanning an item’s barcode, making finding deals simple and fast. This makes it possible for on the spot couponing as a shopper browsing at items at a store could easily scan the barcode of an item they are interested in at that moment to check if there are any available deals.

What separates CouponIt from existing coupon apps, like Groupon, is its focus on developing the app into a social network rather than a platform exclusively for advertisements. This app would allow users to easily share the deals they find with friends and family, which would give more exposure to businesses and their products.
